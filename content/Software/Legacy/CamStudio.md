# CamStudio

**Link:** [https://camstudio.org/](https://camstudio.org/)

**Description:**
Free, open-source software for recording screen and audio activity on your computer. Note: Development has been stagnant for long periods, and unofficial installers often bundle adware. Modern alternatives like OBS Studio are generally recommended.
